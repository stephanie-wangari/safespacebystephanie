Getting started

  • Register / log in — Create an account or sign in. Anonymous crisis chat may still require a lightweight anonymous sign-in for your safety log.
  • Navigation — Use the bottom bar (mobile) or top menu: Home, Tracking, Counsel (chat), Report, Help (this hub).

Home & SOS

  • SOS — Use only when you need immediate campus/security attention. Follow on-screen steps.
  • Quick actions — Shortcuts to report, resources, and safety tools from the home screen.
In immediate danger, call 999 / 112 or campus security 067-5870101. National GBV helpline: 0800 720 990.

Report

  • Submit anonymous or identified reports with narrative, time/location, and optional evidence uploads.
  • Gender Welfare Office (GWO) uses submitted details to follow up outside the chat — not all flows are real-time in-app.

Counsel (chat)

  • The assistant gives trauma-informed guidance grounded in Kenyan law references in the system prompt.
  • Sensitive phrases (e.g. assault, feeling unsafe) may trigger fixed safety messages with hotlines and reporting options so you still get help if the AI service fails.
  • Asking to “talk to a human” explains how to reach GWO in person or via the Report form — staff do not join this chat thread by default.

Tracking

  • Optional location sharing for trusted contacts or responders when you choose to enable it.

Help (resources)

  • Hotlines, legal links, counseling contacts, and JKUAT policy links.
  • Download PDF user guide (replace public/docs/SafeSpace-User-Guide.pdf in the project with your official document).

Privacy

  • Treat your login as sensitive. Review institutional privacy notices for how reports and escalations are stored.
